## About Middle Fork:

Middle Fork River Forest Preserve is a night sky area in Champaign County, IL. It is a designated dark-sky preserve the nonprofit DarkSky International, so you can guarantee that it is an excellent place for stargazing. The forest preserve itself is maintained by another nonprofit company which aims to protect the flora and fauna of the forests of Champaign County. Here's a list of the ones I was able to hear or even observe while I was there. I'd like to be able to someday capture some of these animals on my camera!

### Observed animals

* Deer
* Red fox
* Raccoon
* Opossum

### Heard animals

* Owl
* Frog
* Coyote
